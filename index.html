simple omelette recipe

an easy and quick dish ,perfect for any meal, this classic omelette cambines beaten eggs cooked to perefection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables,or meats.

preparation time

  • total: approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation: 5 minutes
  • cocking: 5 minutes

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • papper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:chesse, diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs
  • 1. beat the eggs:in abowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and peper until they are well mixed . you can add atablespoon of water or milk for fluffier texture
  • 2.heat the pan:place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil .
  • 3.cook the omelette:once the butter is melted and bubbing , pour the eggs ,till the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  • 4.add fillings (optional):when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle , sprinkle your chosen filings over one half of the omelette.
  • 5.fold and serve:as the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings, let it cook for another minute ,then slide it onto a placte.
  • 6.enjoy:serve hot, with additional salt and papper if needed.
